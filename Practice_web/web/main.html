At first we will learn some new words:

  • Wood —— Лес
  • Valley —— Долина
  • Hill —— Холм
  • Huge —— Огромный
  • Tree —— Дерево
  • There was —— Там был...

Example:

On a hill above the valley there was a wood.

In the wood there was a huge tree.
